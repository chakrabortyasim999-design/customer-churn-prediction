Business Problem Statement:
The company wants to understand customer retention behavior and identify which customer cohorts generate the highest long-term revenue.
The objective is to analyze:
•	Monthly customer retention
•	Repeat purchase patterns
•	Customer lifetime value
•	Churn trends
•	Revenue contribution by cohort
This analysis will help leadership:
•	Improve retention strategy
•	Reduce customer churn
•	Optimize marketing spend
•	Increase repeat purchases
•	Identify high-value customer segments
Key Business Questions:
1.	Which acquisition month produces the highest retention?
2.	How quickly do customers churn?
3.	What percentage of customers return after first purchase?
4.	Which customer cohorts generate the highest revenue?
5.	Which countries have the best repeat customers?
6.	What is the average customer lifetime value?
